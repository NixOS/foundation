# Travel policy

If you are approved to travel with the NixOS foundation covering the expenses, the foundation will cover travel and accommodation.

Your accommodation expenses must not exceed the [country-specific nightly limits set forth by the European Commission](https://ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders/opportunities/docs/2021-2027/common/guidance/additional-information-on-unit-costs-and-contributions_en.pdf) (Accommodation for calls with opening date as from 13 May 2025, page 14 through 17).
